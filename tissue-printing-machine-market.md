<p><p>The tissue printing machine market includes various types, predominantly letterpress and intaglio printing machines. Letterpress printing uses raised surfaces to transfer ink onto tissue, suitable for high-quality images and text. Intaglio printing, on the other hand, involves incising designs into a surface, allowing for deeper ink transfer, resulting in richer textures and detailed prints. Both methods cater to diverse applications in producing decorative tissue products, each offering unique advantages in quality, efficiency, and printing capabilities.</p></p>
