<p><p>The metal cutting and grinding fluids market includes four main types: </p><p>1. **Emulsion Fluids** are oil-in-water mixtures that offer cooling and lubrication, ideal for various machining operations.</p><p>2. **Semisynthetic Fluids** blend synthetic and petroleum components, providing enhanced cooling and reduced oil consumption.</p><p>3. **Synthetic Fluids** are fully synthetic formulations that offer superior cooling and lubricating properties without oil, suitable for high-speed machining.</p><p>4. **Neat Oil Fluids** are pure oils used for specific applications requiring high lubrication without emulsifying agents, ensuring excellent surface finish and tool life.</p></p>
