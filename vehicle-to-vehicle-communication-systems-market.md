<p><p>Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) communication systems encompass OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) devices and aftermarket devices. OEM devices are integrated directly into new vehicles during manufacturing, providing seamless connectivity and enhanced safety features. In contrast, aftermarket devices are retrofitted into existing vehicles, allowing drivers to benefit from V2V technology without needing to purchase a new vehicle.
