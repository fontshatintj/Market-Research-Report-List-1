<p><p>The volatile silicone oil market is segmented based on viscosity levels: below 2 centistokes (cs), 2-4 cs, and above 4 cs. Oils with viscosity below 2 cs are typically used in personal care products for lightweight, quick-drying applications. Those with 2-4 cs offer a balance of spreadability and evaporation rate, suitable for cosmetics and skincare. Oils above 4 cs provide a thicker texture, often used in hair care and industrial applications, where longer-lasting effects are desired.</p></p>
