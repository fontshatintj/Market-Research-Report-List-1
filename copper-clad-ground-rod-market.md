<p><p>The copper clad ground rod market features two primary types: single type and sectional ground type. Single type ground rods are solid, continuous rods made of a copper-clad material, providing efficient grounding with minimal installation complexity. In contrast, sectional ground type rods consist of multiple interconnected segments, allowing for flexibility in installation and adaptability to varying soil conditions. Both types serve to enhance electrical grounding systems, ensuring safety and reliability in a range of applications, from residential to industrial use.</p></p>
