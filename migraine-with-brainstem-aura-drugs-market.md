<p><p>The Migraine with Brainstem Aura drug market is categorized into two primary types: abortive and preventive medications. Abortive medications aim to alleviate symptoms during a migraine attack, providing immediate relief when an episode begins. Common examples include triptans and NSAIDs. Preventive medications, on the other hand, are taken regularly to reduce the frequency and severity of migraines, helping to manage overall condition. These may include beta-blockers, anticonvulsants, and certain antidepressants, targeting underlying triggers and minimizing future attacks.</p></p>
