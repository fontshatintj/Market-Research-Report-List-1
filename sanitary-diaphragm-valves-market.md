<p><p>The sanitary diaphragm valves market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Sanitary Threaded Diaphragm Valves** feature threaded connections for easy installation and maintenance, commonly used in smaller pipelines. </p><p>2. **Sanitary Welded Diaphragm Valves** are permanently welded to the piping system, ensuring a leak-proof seal and are ideal for high-purity applications. </p><p>3. **Sanitary Flanged Diaphragm Valves** utilize flanged connections for secure, robust fittings and are suitable for larger systems requiring easy disassembly. Each type is designed to maintain hygiene and control fluid flow in sanitary environments.</p></p>
