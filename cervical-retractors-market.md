<p><strong>The Cervical Retractors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transverse Retractors</li><li>Longitudinal Retractors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cervical retractors market comprises two main types: transverse and longitudinal retractors. Transverse retractors are designed to provide lateral exposure during cervical surgeries by retracting tissue away from the incision site. They enhance visibility and access for surgeons. Longitudinal retractors, on the other hand, are used to manipulate and hold back structures along the length of the cervical area, offering a more extended view of the surgical field. Both types are essential for optimizing surgical outcomes and patient safety.</p></p>
