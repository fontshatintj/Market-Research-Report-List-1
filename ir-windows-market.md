<p><ul><li>Crystal Material</li><li>Polymer Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IR windows market is categorized into two main types: crystal material and polymer material. Crystal materials, like quartz or sapphire, offer high durability and excellent optical transparency for infrared wavelengths, making them ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, polymer materials, such as polyethylene or fluoropolymer, provide lightweight, flexible solutions at a lower cost, suitable for less demanding environments. Both types serve distinct industries, including military, medical, and industrial applications, addressing varying performance and budgetary requirements.</p></p>
