<p><p>The Dental Ultrasonic Surgical System market is categorized into high-frequency and low-frequency systems. High-frequency systems typically operate above 25 kHz, providing precision in cutting and minimization of thermal damage to surrounding tissues, making them ideal for delicate procedures like bone surgery or periodontal treatment. In contrast, low-frequency systems function between 1 and 25 kHz, offering greater power for tougher materials and surgical applications. Both types cater to specific dental needs, enhancing surgical outcomes and patient safety in dentistry.</p></p>
