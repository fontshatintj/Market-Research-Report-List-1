<p><p>The sludge treatment system market encompasses various technologies for processing waste sludge. The Bubbling Fluidized Bed (BFB) incineration system uses a bed of heated particles to combust sludge efficiently. Circulating Fluidized Bed (CFB) incineration offers improved mixing and higher combustion temperatures. Biomass-Coal systems combine biomass with coal for energy recovery. Vacuum dryers remove moisture from sludge under reduced pressure, enhancing drying efficiency. Other technologies may include anaerobic digestion and advanced thermal treatments, providing diverse solutions for sludge management.</p></p>
