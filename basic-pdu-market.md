<p><p>Basic Power Distribution Units (PDUs) are crucial in data centers, with two primary market types: floor-standing and basic rack PDUs. Floor-standing PDUs are independent units that can accommodate larger loads and provide multiple outlets for high-density environments. Basic rack PDUs, on the other hand, are designed to fit within standard server racks, offering efficient power distribution for individual servers and equipment. Both types are essential for optimizing power management and ensuring reliable performance in IT infrastructures.</p></p>
