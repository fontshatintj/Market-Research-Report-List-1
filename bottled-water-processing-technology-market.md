<p><p>The bottled water processing technology market primarily consists of two types: still water and sparkling water. Still water is typically sourced from natural springs or municipal supplies and undergoes filtration, purification, and sometimes mineralization to enhance taste. Sparkling water, on the other hand, is carbonated through natural or artificial methods, providing a fizzy experience. Both types require distinct processing technologies, including treatment methods like reverse osmosis and UV disinfection, to ensure safety, quality, and compliance with health standards before bottling.</p></p>
