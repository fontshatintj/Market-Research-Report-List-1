<p><p>The cast steel globe valves market encompasses various end types for diverse applications. Raised-flange end valves feature a protruding flange, facilitating easier installation and maintenance. Ring-type joint end valves utilize a machined groove for seals, ensuring a tight connection and high pressure resistance. Butt-weld end valves provide a seamless join to pipes, offering a robust solution for high-pressure environments. Each type serves distinct operational needs, making them vital in industries like oil and gas, power generation, and water treatment.</p></p>
