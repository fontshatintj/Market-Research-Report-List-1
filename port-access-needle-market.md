<p><p>The port access needle market comprises three main types: winged port access needles, angled port access needles, and straight port access needles. Winged needles feature plastic "wings" for easier handling and stability during insertion, making them ideal for patients requiring frequent access. Angled needles offer a specific insertion angle, facilitating access to difficult veins, while straight needles provide direct access and are often used in standard procedures. Each type serves distinct clinical applications, catering to various patient needs and healthcare settings.</p></p>
