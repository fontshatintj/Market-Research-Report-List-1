<p><ul><li>Belt-Based Checkweigher</li><li>Roller Conveyor Based Checkweigher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Food and Beverage Checkweigher market is primarily divided into two types: belt-based checkweighers and roller conveyor-based checkweighers. Belt-based checkweighers utilize a moving belt to transport products for accurate weight measurement, ideal for irregular shapes or soft packages. In contrast, roller conveyor-based checkweighers feature rollers that facilitate the movement of rigid containers and pallets, ensuring quick and efficient weighing. Both types play a crucial role in quality control and compliance in food and beverage production, ensuring products meet specified weight standards.</p></p>
