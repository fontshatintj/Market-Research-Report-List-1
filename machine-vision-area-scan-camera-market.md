<p><p>The machine vision camera market primarily consists of line scan and area scan cameras. Line scan cameras capture images by scanning a line of pixels across a scene, making them ideal for long objects and continuous processes, like web inspection. Area scan cameras, on the other hand, capture an entire two-dimensional image in a single frame, suitable for applications requiring detailed visuals of static scenes, such as quality control in manufacturing. Both serve distinct roles in automated imaging solutions.</p></p>
