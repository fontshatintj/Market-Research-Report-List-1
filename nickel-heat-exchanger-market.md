Double tube models feature two concentric tubes, allowing effective heat transfer between two fluids while minimizing contamination risks. Other types may include plate and shell designs or spiral heat exchangers, each providing unique configurations and efficiencies tailored to various industrial applications and challenging environments.</p></p>
