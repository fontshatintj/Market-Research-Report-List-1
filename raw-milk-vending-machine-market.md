<p><p>The raw milk vending machine market can be categorized into two types: with milk tank and without milk tank. Machines with a milk tank are equipped to store and maintain the milk's freshness, offering a regulated supply directly from farms. In contrast, machines without a milk tank typically dispense milk that is pre-packaged or transported in bulk, often requiring immediate consumption. Both types cater to consumers seeking fresh, unprocessed milk, but they differ in how the milk is stored and presented.</p></p>
