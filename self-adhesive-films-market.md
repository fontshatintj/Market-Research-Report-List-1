<p><p>The self-adhesive films market can be categorized into three primary types: translucent, transparent, and opaque. Translucent films allow light to pass through while obscuring visibility, making them ideal for decorative applications. Transparent films are clear and offer visibility while providing protection or branding opportunities, commonly used for labels and window graphics. Opaque films block light completely and are used for privacy, advertising, and covering surfaces. Each type serves unique uses across various industries, including packaging, automotive, and home decor.</p></p>
