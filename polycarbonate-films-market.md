<p><p>The polycarbonate films market is categorized into three main types: optical, flame retardant, and weatherable films. Optical polycarbonate films are designed for high clarity and are commonly used in applications like lenses and displays. Flame retardant films incorporate additives to resist ignition, making them vital for safety in construction and electronics. Weatherable polycarbonate films offer enhanced durability against UV radiation and environmental conditions, ideal for outdoor applications such as signage and protective covers, ensuring longevity and performance.</p></p>
