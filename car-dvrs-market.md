<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car DVRs, or dashboard cameras, are categorized into two main types: Single Channel and Dual Channel. Single Channel DVRs record footage from the front of the vehicle only, making them more affordable and simpler to use. In contrast, Dual Channel DVRs capture video from both the front and rear, providing comprehensive coverage and enhanced security. The Dual Channel market is growing due to increasing demand for safety features and complete accident documentation, while Single Channel models appeal to budget-conscious consumers.</p></p>
