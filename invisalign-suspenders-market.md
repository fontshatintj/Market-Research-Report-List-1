<p><p>The Invisalign suspenders market comprises three primary types: lingual suspenders, clear aligners, and ceramic suspenders. Lingual suspenders are fixed behind the teeth, offering a discreet orthodontic solution. Clear aligners are removable and designed for comfort, providing a nearly invisible way to straighten teeth. Ceramic suspenders, made from tooth-colored materials, blend with the natural teeth, making them less noticeable than traditional metal braces. Each type offers unique aesthetic and functional benefits to cater to different patient preferences and needs.</p></p>
