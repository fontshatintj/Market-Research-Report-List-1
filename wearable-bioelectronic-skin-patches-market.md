<p><p>Wearable bioelectronic skin patches utilize various sensor types to monitor health and physiological parameters. Electrophysiological sensors detect electrical signals from the body, useful for heart and neural activity monitoring. Biochemical sensors analyze blood or interstitial fluid for biomarkers, aiding in disease management. Potentiometric sensors measure ion concentrations, offering insights into electrolyte balance. Tactile sensors perceive pressure and texture, enabling real-time feedback on touch interactions. Together, these sensors enhance health tracking and personalized care through advanced wearable technology.</p></p>
