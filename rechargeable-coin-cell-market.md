<p><p>The rechargeable coin cell market is characterized by two main types: ML (Manganese Lithium) and LIR (Lithium Ion Rechargeable) cells. ML cells offer stable voltage and are suitable for powering low-energy devices, such as watches and sensors. LIR cells, on the other hand, provide higher energy density and longer life cycles, making them ideal for applications like smart wearables and compact electronic devices. Both types cater to the growing demand for sustainable power solutions in various consumer and industrial electronics.</p></p>
