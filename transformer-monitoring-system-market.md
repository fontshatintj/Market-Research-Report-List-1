<p><p>The Transformer Monitoring System market includes various types focused on enhancing the reliability and efficiency of transformers. Bushing monitoring involves assessing the condition of transformer bushings to prevent insulation failures. DGA (Dissolved Gas Analysis) devices detect gas levels in transformer oil, indicating potential faults. Partial discharge (PD) monitoring identifies electrical discharges within insulation systems that can lead to failures. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses various emerging technologies and solutions that aid in monitoring transformer health and performance.</p></p>
