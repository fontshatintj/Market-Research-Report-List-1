<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Relay</li><li>Solid State Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The I/O relays market comprises primarily two types: electromagnetic relays and solid-state relays. Electromagnetic relays utilize an electromechanical design that uses magnetism for switching actions, making them ideal for high-capacity loads and simpler control applications. In contrast, solid-state relays employ semiconductor technology for faster switching, higher reliability, and greater endurance in demanding environments. Both types serve critical roles in automation, control systems, and other industrial applications, ensuring efficient performance and electrical isolation.</p></p>
