<p><ul><li>Mechanical Dehumidifier</li><li>Electronic Dehumidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interior dehumidifiers market features two main types: mechanical and electronic dehumidifiers. Mechanical dehumidifiers utilize a refrigeration cycle to condense moisture from the air, making them effective for large spaces and high humidity levels. They are commonly used in basements and industrial settings. In contrast, electronic dehumidifiers employ thermoelectric technology, offering a quieter and more energy-efficient solution for smaller areas like bedrooms or bathrooms. Both types serve to maintain optimal humidity levels, improving comfort and preventing mold growth.</p></p>
